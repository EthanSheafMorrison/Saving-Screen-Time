Successful candidates will explore critical issues at the intersection of architecture, urbanism, and community.

THE HOUSE

Lord Richard and Lady Ruth Rogers gifted the Wimbledon House to Harvard GSD. Rogers designed the house in the late 1960s as a pioneering example of high-tech architecture, and it continues to serve as a place of study and reflection for future generations of designers.

Team

Practice
